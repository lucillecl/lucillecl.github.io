EDUCATE360

The purpose of EDUCATE360 is to provide free help for students who lack traditional academic and social-emotional support. We offer tutoring in various subjects and assist in services, such as STEM tutoring, college advice, essay editing, and many more!
Get Started!


The 4 S's

  1. Search: Go on our tutors page and look through our list of qualified college helpers
  2. Select: Pick your preferred tutor according to your needs and goals
  3. Schedule: fill our tutor request form (located on the top of the tutors page)
  4. Study: receive a scheduled appointment time that works for both you and your tutor, click on the zoom/google hangouts link provided, and happy studying!
