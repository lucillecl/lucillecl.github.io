EDUCATE360 Goals

In EDUCATE360, we aim to provide free private and group tutoring to all students, with a focus on marginalized students, students with dis/abilities, and students affected by the COVID pandemic.

EDUCATE360 aims to see students from diverse racial, ethnic, economic, and regional background exceeding in education. We work to achieve this goal by tutoring students online for free.

EDUCATE360 aims to cultivate a culture of learning, academic excellence, and positive social-emotional growth to increase educational outcomes, knowledge, and life skills for all.

Background Of EDUCATE360

EDUCATE360 was founded in 2020 amidst the COVID-19 pandemic after Lucille noticed a number of external factors, such as social inequality, economic disparity, and/or political situations, which unfairly hindered children from reaching their full potentials. After hours of brainstorming and preparation, she started this non-profit tutoring website that is free for any student to use!
